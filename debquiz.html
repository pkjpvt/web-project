Debate & Quiz club

The Debate and the Quiz Club strongly believes in the words, “Knowledge is Power”, and with a power as strong, it should be perfected into a useful tool for the welfare of the society. Today’s youth and tomorrow’s future.

Activity

In our club, public speaking and knowledge sharing is encouraged through the various events held by the club throughout the year. Some of the common events are Debate Competition, Youth Parliament, Quiz Competition, etc.

Achievement

The club organises many events and competitions and have many achivements. The Club have won many awards during inter-college competitions. Students that take part in the events were given the awards and certificates.
