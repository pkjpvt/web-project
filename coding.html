Coding Club

It is one of the oldest clubs of Assam Don Bosco University. Coding can be defined as the process of writing instructions in a specific computer language that tells the computer what to do. Coding helps in developing critical thinking and problem solving skills. Coding is one of the most demanded skills in recent times as we are living in a digital age. Here, in the coding club, we try to guide new students in the field of programming and coding. We organise various competitive events to create awareness and a positive atmosphere about this field, and we also organise various workshops to educate new students and guide them.

Activity

The Coding Club helps students develop programming skills and inspires them to pursue future programming and other digital careers. The coding clubs helps generate the participant to enchance their knownledge in the coding. By taking part in the activtity they can learn more. Such as:

  • Try a new language as a group
  • Code a project for social good
  • Make a game together
  • Take a class with your club
  • Explore your hobbies in code
  • Participate in a high school hackathon

Achivement

It teaches not only how the programming language works, but also how to use it for a specific purpose based on the field the student wishes to pursue. Getting into a career in coding is a smart move. It’s one of the fastest growing professions today, and ranks as one of the top 10 highest paying computer science jobs in 2022. But the computer programming career path is also a competitive, demanding, and ever changing career. This means that it’s important to stay up to date, creative, and versatile in your own mastery of the subject. One of the best ways to get good at coding, and we mean really good, is by involving it more frequently in your life, not just while studying. You’ll gain a whole host of benefits and boost your career by taking part in extracurricular activities which involve coding in some regard.
